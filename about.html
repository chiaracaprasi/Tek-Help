About

Chiara Caprasi is the Founder and Director of Tek Help.

In creating Tek Help, Chiara has combined her two passions, love of technology and the desire to help people, particularly women of all ages.

She is passionate about removing ‘tech frustration’ from women’s lives so that using technology becomes a productive and enjoyable experience.

Chiara is also committed to training women to feel empowered in a digital world.

Originally from Cinque Terre, Italy, Chiara (pronounced ‘Kiahra’) has worked internationally for over a decade as project coordinator and IT professional. She now lives in Melbourne, Australia.

Some fun facts:

  • Chiara has a black cat, adopted from the Lost Dogs Home, who loves chasing the computer’s mouse!🐈‍⬛
  • Chiara is a regular practitioner of yoga and meditation and a passionate swimmer. 🏊‍♀️
  • And she loves cooking Italian food, of course! 🍝
